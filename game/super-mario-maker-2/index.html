Super Mario Maker 2

Summary

Super Mario Maker 2 is currently in a playable state.

In order to have the best possible experience, please follow the instructions below:

  • Dump and use your NAND and keys from your console following our quickstart guide.
  • Under Controls, use either the Pro Controller or Dual Joycons controller options along with setting the Console Mode to Docked.

As with most emulators, your in-game performance is highly dependent on the power of your system.

IN-GAME ISSUES

  • Fonts are missing on AMD and Intel GPUs.
  • The twister’s particle is broken in the Super Mario Bros., Super Mario Bros. 3 and Super Mario World game styles with the little air bubbles stuck underneath the twister. The error is not present in underwater, New Super Mario Bros. U, and Super Mario 3D World styles.
  • In the snow theme of the Super Mario Bros., Super Mario Bros. 3 and Super Mario World game styles, some snowfall particles are broken; in daytime they stop moving after a certain moment, while during night-time they are glitched.
  • Since online is not and will never be implemented, the course world will kick you back to the previous menu that you were on.

There exists a save file editor, which will let you download courses from SMMDB. The editor is an external program and the game should not be running in yuzu while editing. The courses will be put into your course bot.
