DAEMON X MACHINA™

Summary

Daemon X Machina is currently in a playable state utilizing the OpenGL and Vulkan API. However, only the Vulkan API shows the proper text in-game and through the menus.

Encountered Issues:

  • OpenGL specific:
  • Menu text is missing and makes navigating the entire menu system extremely difficult.
  • Major textures and some effects are missing in and out of gameplay.
  • Some slight slowdowns occur when specific enemies known as “Immortals” appear on screen in two or more quantities.
  • Vulkan specific:
  • Major textures and some effects are missing in and out of gameplay.
  • Game can freeze when facing two or more enemy “Immortals”. The slowdown experienced during this encounter is more than OpenGL.

General Information:

  • Test Bed Platform:
  • Windows 10 Professional
  • CPU: Intel i9 9900k @ 4.7 GHz
  • GPU: NVIDIA 1080 Ti
  • SSD: Samsung NVME Drive
  • Yuzu Build: Early Access 1703 (2021-05-21)
  • OpenGL & Vulkan Renderer Tested
  • The entire game works great in the OpenGL renderer outside of the missing texture and text issues.
  • Spotty audio issues are only present during some cutscenes but audio plays back well in gameplay.
  • Hub area works perfectly.

In order to navigate into the game and try the first mission under OpenGL, please follow the instructions below:

  • Once the game loads a white box with a black oval will be seen. Press “A”, this will select “English”.
  • A second white box and black oval will appear, press A again.
  • The game will now load the title screen.
  • Press “A” on the title screen to reveal a white rectangular selection box.
  • Press “A” again and this will select “New Game”.
  • Intro video will play with small audio glitches.
  • Character creation will be shown after the intro video. Press “Up” to skip to the bottom and press “A” to continue.
  • Enter a name and press the “OK” key on the screen.
  • Press “A” to confirm when the white box and black oval appears. You should now see a video of your character.
  • Walk up to the central computer terminal and press “A”.
  • Navigation of terminal is:
  1. Missions
  2. Hangar
  3. System
  • Press “A” on the top position.
  • Press “A” again to confirm.
  • Press “A” four times to start the first mission.
  • Launch video will play.
  • Continue pressing “A” to clear the dialogue sequence.
  • Gameplay of the first mission will commence.
